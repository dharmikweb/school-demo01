About Evergreen

An education rooted in character, built for the future

Evergreen International School was founded in 1998 on a simple belief: children learn best when they feel safe, seen, and steadily challenged. Today, across our Junior, Middle, and Senior Schools, that belief still shapes every timetable, every classroom, and every conversation with a parent.

We are a CBSE-affiliated day school on a 6-acre green campus, combining a rigorous academic core with the arts, sport, and community service — because well-rounded isn't a slogan here, it's a schedule.

  • CBSE curriculum enriched with global learning frameworks
  • Low student-to-teacher ratio across all sections
  • Dedicated counselling, health, and safety infrastructure
  • Purpose-built labs, library, sports and arts facilities

Academics

A curriculum that grows with the child

From first letters to board examinations, each stage is designed around how children actually learn at that age.

Play-based Foundations

Structured play, phonics, motor-skill activities, and story-led learning build the foundation for reading, number sense, and social confidence — all in cheerful, sensory-rich classrooms.

  • Montessori-inspired activity corners
  • Daily music, movement & story time
  • 1 teacher + 1 assistant per 15 children

Building Strong Fundamentals

CBSE core subjects are paired with hands-on science, coding basics, and creative writing labs, so concepts are understood — not just memorised.

  • Activity-based Math & EVS labs
  • Reading circles & library periods
  • Introductory coding & robotics

Widening the Lens

Students move between subject specialists, take on project-based assessments, and choose their first electives in art, music, or applied technology.

  • Subject-specialist teaching model
  • STEM & design-thinking projects
  • House-based leadership programme

Board-Ready, Future-Ready

Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams with dedicated board-prep mentors, career counselling, and university application guidance from Grade IX onward.

  • Science / Commerce / Humanities streams
  • Weekly mentor check-ins from Grade IX
  • University & career counselling cell
